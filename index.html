How my ratings work

My name is Noncompliant and these are tunes in my crate.

To understand my ratings, you have to know that I am only putting stuff up
that I like and that I put in my crate. There are no “bad” reviews here.
Ain’t nobody got time for that. This is just the way I rate things that end up in the crate.
